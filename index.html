Welcome to Ross Bakery

At Ross Bakery, we pride ourselves on serving freshly baked goods made from family recipes handed down for generations. Come enjoy our warm, cozy café in the heart of Ross, Ohio, where every treat is made with care.

Our Favorites

Our bakery offers a wide variety of freshly baked items every day. Some of our most popular treats include:

  • Chocolate-glazed donuts and classic varieties
  • Flaky croissants and sweet rolls
  • Custom cakes for birthdays and special occasions
  • Seasonal pastries and pies

Stop by to taste our daily specials or place a custom order for your next event!
