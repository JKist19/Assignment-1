Our History

Founded in the heart of Ross, Ohio, Ross Bakery began as a small, family-owned shop with a simple goal: to bring fresh, homemade baked goods to our community each day. What started as a neighborhood bakery quickly became a local favorite, known for the comforting aroma of bread in the morning and the friendly faces behind the counter. Over the years, we have continued to grow while staying true to our roots—serving generations of families with recipes passed down and perfected with care.

Our Mission

At Ross Bakery, our mission is to provide high-quality baked goods made from fresh, carefully selected ingredients. We are committed to creating a warm and welcoming environment where every customer feels like part of our family. From handcrafted breads and pastries to custom cakes for special occasions, we take pride in delivering products that not only taste exceptional but also bring people together. Quality, community, and tradition are at the heart of everything we bake.
